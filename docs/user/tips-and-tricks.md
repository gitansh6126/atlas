# Tips & Tricks

Get the most out of Atlas with these productivity tips and best practices.

## 🚀 Speed Up Your Workflow

- **Master the slash menu**: Memorize your most-used block commands (`/heading`, `/list`, `/card`) to insert them in seconds.
- **Use keyboard shortcuts**: Formatting and navigation shortcuts save significant time once they become muscle memory.
- **Drag and drop**: Reorder pages in the sidebar and blocks on the page by dragging.

## 🃂 Organize Effectively

- **Nest pages**: Use child pages to create a hierarchy instead of a flat list.
- **Use tags and labels**: Categorize important items so you can scan and find them quickly.
- **Name pages clearly**: Descriptive titles make search much more effective.

## ⚡ Boost Productivity

- **Work in one workspace per context**: Keep work, personal, and hobby notes separate to reduce mental clutter.
- **Use templates**: Create a "Template" page with your most common block setup, then duplicate it for new pages.
- **Pin frequently accessed pages**: Keep important pages at the top of your sidebar.

## ✨ Power User Moves

- **HTML Embed for widgets**: Use custom HTML/CSS blocks to embed live clocks, countdowns, or styled info boxes.
- **Kanban for projects**: Track tasks visually with a kanban board.
- **Dark mode for focus**: Switch to dark theme during long sessions to reduce eye strain.

---

> 💡 **Tip**: The best Atlas workflow is the one you stick with. Start simple and evolve your setup over time.

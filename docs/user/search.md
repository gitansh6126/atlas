# Search

Atlas provides a **full-text search** across all pages in your workspace, making it easy to find anything in seconds.eads

## How to Open Search

- **Shortcut**: `Ctrl + Shift + F` (macOS: `Cmd + Shift + F`)
- Or click the **🔍 Search** icon in the top toolbar

---

## Searching Pages

1. Open the search bar
2. Type your keyword or phrase
3. Atlas shows a list of matching pages in real time

Each result shows:
- **Page title**
- **Matching snippet** with the keyword highlighted

Click a result to jump directly to that page.

---

## Search Tips

- 🔤 Type partial words — Atlas matches as you type
- 🔤 Searches are **not case-sensitive** (`Atlas` matches `atlas`)
- 🔤 Search covers all **page titles and content**

---

> 💡 **Tip**: Use unique, specific keywords to narrow down results quickly.

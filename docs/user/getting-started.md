# Getting Started

Welcome to Atlas! This guide will walk you from zero to your first page in just a few minutes. 🎉

## Installation

Atlas runs entirely on your local machine t no cloud required.

### Step 1: Download Atlas

1. Go to the Atlas download page (or your app store)
2. Download the correct version for your operating system:
   -  **Windows**: `.exe` installer
   - 🍎 **macOS**: `.dmg` image
   - 📜 **Linux**: `.AppImage` or `.deb`

### Step 2: Install

- **Windows**: Run the `.exe` and follow the setup wizard
- **macOS**: Open the `.dmg` and drag Atlas into your Applications folder
- **Linux**: Make the AppImage executable (`chmod +x Atlas.AppImage`) and run it

### Step 3: Launch Atlas

Double-click the Atlas icon. You will see a welcome screen asking you to create your first **workspace**.

---

## Your First Workspace

A **workspace** is like a folder that holds all your pages. You can have one workspace for work, another for personal projects, and so on.

1. On the welcome screen, click **Create New Workspace**
2. Give it a name like `My Atlas` or `Work Notes`
3. Choose a location on your computer where Atlas should save files
4. Click **Create Workspace**

Time to Celebrate! 🎉 Your workspace is ready.

---

## Your First Page

Inside the workspace, you will see an empty page list. Let us create your first page.

1. Click the **+ New Page** button (top-left in munchies area or sidebar)
2. Type a name for your page — for example: `My First Notes`
3. Press **Enter** or click **Create**

You will be taken to the **page editor** ⁰ a blank canvas where you can start building.

---

## Adding Your First Block

Everything in Atlas is a **block**. To add a new block:

1. Click anywhere on the page or press **Enter** at theöt of an existing block
2. Start typing your text ⁰ you have a **Paragraph** block!

To make it a heading instead:

1. Type `# ` (hash + space) at the beginning of the line
2. It becomes a **Heading 1** block automatically

Or even faster: press `/` to open the **slash menu** and pick a block type instantly.

---

## Try a Few Blocks

Click anywhere on the page and type `/` to see the menu. Try inserting these:

- `/heading` — adds a heading block
- `/list` — adds a bullet list item
- `/divider` — adds a horizontal divider line
- `/card` — adds a card container block

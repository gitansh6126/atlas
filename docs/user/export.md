# Export

Atlas gives you options to get your content out of the app for sharing, backup, or publishing.

## Current Export Options

| Format | Availability | Description |
|--------|-------------|-------------|
| **Markdown** | ✅ Available | Clean, portable text with formatting |
| **PDF** | ⚠️ Future | Printable document output |
| **HTML** | ⚠️ Future | Web-ready page export |

---

## Exporting a Page as Markdown

1. Open the page you want to export
2. Click **··· More** → **Export** → **Markdown**
3. Choose where to save the `.md` file
4. Click **Save**

Your page content is converted to standard Markdown, preserving headings, lists, links, and formatting.

---

## Why Export?

- 🔄 **Backup**: Keep a copy outside Atlas
- 🤝 **Share**: Send a page to someone without Atlas
- 🌐 **Publish**: Use Markdown in blogs, GitHub, or other platforms

---

## Future Plans

The Atlas team is working on:

- 💰 **PDF Export**: One-click printable pages
- 🌐 **HTML Export**: Self-contained web pages with styles

---

> 💡 **Tip**: Markdown export is the best way to preserve your content in a widely-compatible format.

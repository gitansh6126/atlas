# Pages

Pages are where your content lives inside a workspace. Each page is a blank canvas made of blocks.

## Creating a Page

1. In the sidebar, click **+ New Page**
2. Type a name for your page
3. Press **Enter** or click **Create**

Your new page opens in the editor.

---

## Renaming a Page

### Method 1: From the Sidebar

1. Right-click a page in the sidebar
2. Select **Rename**
3. Type the new name and press **Enter**

### Method 2: From the Page Editor

1. Click the page title at the top of the editor
2. Type the new name
3. Press **Enter** or click outside the title

---

## Deleting a Page

1. Right-click a page in the sidebar
2. Select **Delete**
3. Confirm the warning

⚠️ **Warning**: Deleted pages cannot be recovered.

---

## Organizing Pages

### Reordering

Drag and drop pages in the sidebar to reorder them.

### Nesting

You can nest pages inside other pages to create a hierarchy:

1. Drag a page onto another page in the sidebar
2. It becomes a **child page**
3. Click the arrow next to a parent page to expand or collapse its children

---

## Folders

While Atlas does not have traditional folders, nested pages act as containers:

- A page with children functions like a folder
- You can view all child pages by opening the parent
